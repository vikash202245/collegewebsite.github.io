President's Message



Technical education has under gone sea change during a last one decade. Thanks to the vision of The College of Technical Courses who took the lead by establishing degree courses as a self financing college in the state of Uttar Pradesh offering courses in engineering , bachelor in science , bachelor in technology and and masters in business administration. With highly committed management, excellent infrastructure, well qualified faculty and competent staff, most disciplined and talented students, this college shall be a torch bearer of tomorrow to show the path to the other colleges in the region.The college is situated in pollution free lush green environment, congenial atmosphere for all around development of budding technologists.The college has the state of the art laboratory and ultra modern computer center with Wi-Fi facility and around the clock internet Connectivity with high speed multimedia teaching system along with digital smart boards are provided in the lecture rooms giving online class notes to the students .attendance and academic performance of the students is uploaded on web system for the information of students and their parents. The most modern library is well stocked with books, journals, digital learning material and online technical and management journals. Students are provided with ample scope for participation in co-curricular and extracurricular activities apart from workshops, guest lectures and seminars which are conducted on regular basis.
